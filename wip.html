D3 Sunburst

This is one of my first experiments with D3.js

Inspired by the windrose featured on D3js.org, this is a sunburst that shows relative volume by the length of each burst. Color indicates a second dimension and each burst has a label that is positioned on the outside.

Party On

We had a holiday party and in addition to the booze, the hot girls and robotic dudes we knew there was something missing.

As technology consultants we always look for ways to show off our skills so a party app was born. During various gatherings ideas were collected, treasurehunt, microlocations, drinking score, TRON, iBeacons, Rasberry Pi, D3...

And there it was. How cool would it be if we could change the behaviour of our guests with something that we hacked together in a couple of weeks... If we could just keep track of how frequently someone would attend the bar, hide some treasures to be found with a smart phone and display it all on a big screen. Visualize what was goig on in real time and hope people get into it.

And that is just what we did... and yes, the people got into it.
